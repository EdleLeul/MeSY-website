Founding Organizations

Meet the organizations that laid the foundation for Media Smart Youth Ethiopia

Eshet Children and Youth Development Organization

ECYDO is a non-governmental organization dedicated to improving opportunities for children and youth. Originally established as a Theatre and Music Club in 1987, it now focuses on social and economic development for young people.

Read More

Institute of International Education - Ethiopia

IIE has over 30 years of experience in Sub-Saharan Africa, implementing programs in leadership development, organizational effectiveness, and higher education scholarship administration to build capacity across the region.

Read More

MahibereHiwot for Social Development

MSD was established by volunteer youth in 1999 to combat HIV/AIDS. Registered in 2002, it has primarily focused on HIV prevention, care & support, and reproductive health programs to serve communities in need.

Read More

Hiwot Ethiopia

Founded in 1995 as a youth club, Hiwot Ethiopia has grown into a registered CSO delivering multi-year programs focused on HIV/AIDS prevention and sexual reproductive health for children and young people across Ethiopia.

Read More

Save Your Holy Land Association

SYHLA focuses on health, education, tourism and environmental interventions. Since 2001, it has implemented various projects in Lalibela and expanded to multiple districts in North Wollo Zone, Amhara Region.

Read More

Voluntary Services Overseas

VSO is an international development charity with a vision for "a fair world for everyone." They create lasting change through volunteering, working in 35 countries across Africa and Asia with international, national, and community volunteers.

Read More

Young Christian Men's Association

YMCA Ethiopia, established in 1951, is a pioneer youth-focused development organization with a vision for empowering young people. Affiliated with the worldwide YMCA movement, it follows holistic youth development principles.

Read More

New Bright Community Development Center

This organization is dedicated to creating positive, sustainable change through empowerment programs focused on education, life skills, and community development to uplift vulnerable groups and inspire self-reliance.

Read More
