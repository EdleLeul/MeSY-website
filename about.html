About Media Smart Youth Ethiopia

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team behind our youth-led media literacy initiatives

Our Story

Media Smart Youth (MeSY) Ethiopia is a direct, proactive, and architecturally unique response to Ethiopia's information crisis. We are a non-governmental, non-partisan consortium built on a radical principle: that the solution to a youth-targeted problem must be entirely youth-led.

Our distinction lies in our structure. MeSY is not just guided by youth input; it is managed and operated by youth at every level of leadership. This includes our project managers, our managing directors, our strategic advisors, and our finance personnel.

This ensures that every strategic decision, every creative initiative, and every operational detail is informed by the lived experience, innovation, and urgency of the very generation we serve.

Our Approach

We anchor our work in the principles of positive youth development. Instead of a fear-based model, we take a positive approach to media literacy, encouraging young people to safely explore, create, and critique media while harnessing its vast opportunities for good.

We design flexible, participatory programs that respect and build upon the existing knowledge and digital experiences of young people. Our curriculum is not theoretical; it is applied, equipping youth with hands-on skills they can use immediately.

We leverage the power of our consortium by implementing programs through our member organizations and their vast youth networks. This ensures deep community reach and lasting impact.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

MeSY works to advance young peoples' active citizenship and democratic participation in Ethiopia through the promotion of media literacy. Our goal is to empower young people with skills to access, critically analyze, and evaluate media and enable them to be more responsible in creating and sharing media messages.

Our Vision

We envision an Ethiopia where all young people are equipped with the media literacy skills needed to navigate the digital landscape responsibly, critically evaluate information, and become positive contributors to their communities and the nation's democratic processes.

Our Values

Youth leadership, integrity, innovation, collaboration, and empowerment guide everything we do. We believe in the power of young people to drive change and create a more informed, peaceful, and democratic society.
