About Us

Media Smart Youth (MeSY) Ethiopia is a youth-powered ecosystem dedicated to building resilience against misinformation. We are a non-governmental, non-partisan consortium built on the principle that the solution to youth-targeted misinformation must be entirely youth-led.

In Ethiopia, a nation with a median age of 19.5, the recent expansion of digital media has created a potent double-edged sword. While it offers unprecedented opportunities for connection and activism, it has simultaneously become a primary battleground for information warfare.

Our distinction lies in our structure. MeSY is not just guided by youth input; it is managed and operated by youth at every level of leadership. This ensures that every strategic decision is informed by the lived experience of the very generation we serve.
